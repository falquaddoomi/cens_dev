About the ASAP Pilot

Thank you for agreeing to participate in the Adolescent & Young Adult Survivorship Action Plan (ASAP) Text Messaging Study. Taking part in this study includes three steps: (1) reading an online educational booklet, (2) picking your ASAP goals and (3) text-messaging. You will now have the chance to look through and read the ASAP booklet. Once you have clicked through the pages of the ASAP booklet you will be asked to choose 3 ASAP goals to help with your survivorship care.

Over the next few weeks you will receive texted information and questions from our text messaging system to help you complete your ASAP goals. You will start with one goal before you move onto the next. Text messaging will end after you complete your 3 ASAP goals or after two months, whichever comes first.

At the end of the study you will receive a post-test questionnaire. Once we receive your post-test a research member will call you to talk about your experience with the text messaging system. This should last about 20 minutes. After the call we will mail out your gift card as a thank you for your participation.
